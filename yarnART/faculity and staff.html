Note: Faculty appointments in each area of graduate study correspond to varying levels of engagement at the School of Art. Core Faculty: On campus regularly each week for courses, critiques, studio visits, advising and other service. Core faculty also includes the Directors and Assistant Directors for each area of study. Appointed Faculty: Four or more days in one semester for studio visits, critiques, and/or teaching a course or workshop. Visiting Critics: Three days in one semester for studio visits, critiques, and/or workshops
